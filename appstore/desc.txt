MicroAC is an audio converter for Core Audio formats for iOS and Mac OS X.

It's a GUI version of the "afconvert" utility on Mac OS X. It supports most
input formats and most output formats that afconvert supports.

Features:
-Drag And Drop
-Concurrency control
-Queueing & Requeueing
-Sample Rate
-Channels

Formats:
3gp (.3gp)
  aac, aace, aacf, aach, aacl, aacp

3gp2 (.3gp2)
  aac, aace, aacf, aach, aacl, aacp

AIFC (.aifc, .aiff, .aif)
  ima4, PCM I8, PCM UI8, PCM BEI16,
  PCM BEI24 PCM BEI32, PCM BEIF32, PCM BEIF64

AIFF (.aiff, .aif)
  PCM I8, PCM UI8, PCM BEI16, PCM BEI24 PCM BEI32

Apple MPEG-4 Audio (.m4a, .m4r)
  aac, aace, aacf, aach, aacl, aacp

Apple MPEG-4 AudioBooks (.m4b)
  aac, aace, aacf, aach, aacl, aacp

CAFF (.caf)
  aac, aace, aacf, aach, aacl, aacp, ilbc,
  ima4, PCM I8, PCM BEI16, PCM BEI24 PCM BEI32,
  PCM BEIF32, PCM BEIF64, PCM LEI16, PCM LEI32,
  PCM LEF32, PCM LEF64

MPEG-4 Audio (.mp4)
  aac, aace, aacf, aach, aacl, aacp

WAVE (.wav)
  PCM I8, PCM BEI16, PCM BEI24 PCM BEI32, PCM BEIF32,
  PCM BEIF64
